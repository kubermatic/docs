## Supported Operating Systems

The following operating systems are supported:

* Ubuntu 18.04 (Bionic)
* Ubuntu 20.04 (Focal)
* CentOS 7
* CentOS 8
* RHEL 8.0, 8.1, 8.2, 8.3\*, 8.4\*
* Flatcar
* Amazon Linux 2

\* RHEL 8.3 and 8.4 are supported only with KubeOne 1.3.3 and newer.
